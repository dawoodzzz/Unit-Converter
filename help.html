👤 Account & Features

Do I need to create an account?
No, the basic conversion tool works without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your conversion history and preferences.
How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation bar, fill in your information (name, email, password), and click "Create Account". You'll be logged in automatically.
How do I save my conversion history?
When logged in, all your conversions are automatically saved to your history. You can see them in the History panel on the main page. Click any history item to quickly repeat that conversion.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can log out at any time. If you want to permanently delete your account data, please contact our support team.

💡 Tips & Tricks

Quick Conversions

Once you've made a conversion, the result is displayed with a copy button. Click it to quickly copy the result to your clipboard!

History Panel

The history panel on the right side shows your recent conversions. Click any item to instantly repeat that conversion with the same values and units.

Mobile Friendly

Unit Converter works perfectly on mobile devices. On smaller screens, the history panel automatically hides to give you more space for conversions.

Keyboard Navigation

Use the Tab key to navigate between fields and the Enter key to quickly convert.

🔧 Troubleshooting

My conversion result looks wrong
Make sure you've selected the correct units to convert FROM and TO. Double-check that you've entered the value correctly. All conversions are based on standard mathematical formulas, so accuracy is guaranteed.
I can't log in to my account
Make sure you're using the correct email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive. If you forgot your password, please contact support.
My history is empty
If you're not logged in, history is temporary and stored locally. Once you refresh the page without logging in, it's cleared. Create an account and log in to save your history permanently.
The website looks broken on my device
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. Make sure you're using a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. If the problem persists, please contact support.
